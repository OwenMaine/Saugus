OUR EXPERTISE

Since 1991, Saugus Construction Corporation has specialized in structural steel erection, serving Georgetown, MA, and surrounding areas. Our team of skilled professionals brings decades of experience in industrial and commercial steel construction, delivering projects that stand the test of time.

OUR MISSION

We are committed to delivering excellence in structural steel erection through uncompromising safety standards, superior craftsmanship, and innovative solutions. Our goal is to be the most trusted partner in steel construction, ensuring every project's success through precision, reliability, and professional integrity.

SAFETY FIRST

Safety is our top priority. We maintain rigorous safety protocols, continuous training, and comprehensive site supervision to ensure zero incidents on every project.

SUPERIOR QUALITY

Our certified welders and experienced ironworkers deliver precision craftsmanship, adhering to the highest industry standards and specifications.

PROVEN EXPERIENCE

With over 30 years of experience, we've successfully completed numerous industrial and commercial steel construction projects across New England.
